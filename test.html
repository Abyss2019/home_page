Welcome to the VRockLab calculation website!

This website archives algorithms we developed for petrological studies, including a few online calculators for apatite ("ApThermo", "ApTimer") and python codes for conducting other calculations ("Python code").

By clicking the buttons below, you will be directed to a new page for calculations. Please cite these software/codes when publishing your work.

ApThermo

This model can be used to calculate the abundances of water in silicate melts using concentrations of volatiles (F, Cl, H2O) in apatite.

Open

ApTimer

ApTimer is developed for estimating the timescale of multicomponent (F-Cl-OH) diffusion in apatite, and it can be applied to natural apatite that is zoned of F/Cl/OH. The calculated diffusion times can be related to magmatic processes such as magma ascent.

Open
